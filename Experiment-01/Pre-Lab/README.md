## Questions and Answers

### 1. What is Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)?

**Answer:**

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is the process of examining, summarizing, and visualizing a dataset to understand its structure and characteristics before applying machine learning algorithms. It helps identify missing values, outliers, patterns, and relationships among variables.

---

### 2. What are the uses of NumPy and Pandas?

**Answer:**

#### NumPy
- Performs numerical computations efficiently.
- Supports multidimensional arrays.
- Provides mathematical and statistical functions.
- Used for scientific computing.

#### Pandas
- Loads datasets from different file formats.
- Organizes data into DataFrames.
- Cleans and preprocesses data.
- Performs data analysis and manipulation.

---

### 3. What is the difference between univariate and multivariate analysis?

**Answer:**

| Univariate Analysis | Multivariate Analysis |
|---------------------|-----------------------|
| Studies a single variable. | Studies two or more variables. |
| Used to understand data distribution. | Used to understand relationships between variables. |
| Example: Histogram of Age. | Example: Scatter Plot of Height vs Weight. |

---

### 4. What are mean, median, standard deviation, and correlation?

**Answer:**

**Mean**
- The average value of a dataset.

**Median**
- The middle value after sorting the data.

**Standard Deviation**
- Measures how much the values are spread around the mean.

**Correlation**
- Measures the strength and direction of the relationship between two variables.
